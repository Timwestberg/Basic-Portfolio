About Me

My name is Tim Westberg, I am a in-training full stack developer, my primary work skills up to this point are in the field of bookkeeping, sales, and business administration. Although my options for my current field are vast I do not have the passion to pursue a career within it. I have been interested in coding since I was 15, I dabbled in game code, mainly picking apart files and seeing what changed within the application. This curiosity grew over the years as I had the opportunity to gain more exposure to web development until I was finally able to get into the UCSD coding camp which I am now attending. My goal going forward is to build effective, solid web applications that effectively manage and operate the vast amount of data small business receive every day.
